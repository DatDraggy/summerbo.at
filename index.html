An exclusive Boat party departing from the Estrel

Hot Summer Nights is an exclusive party on a large boat. From the Estrel dock to the Spree and back, through the German Capital, take in the sunset and mingle on the upper deck, retreat to the lounge and dancefloor with live DJs.

Drinks

On the boat we have a fully equipped bar with all kinds of drinks available. You wanna go for a cold beer from the tap to watch Berlin? Sure, we have it! Want something refreshing after fursuiting or dancing hard? Also no problem! You can pay directly at the bar in cash so no need for tokens or special currency.

Fursuits

Do boats and fursuits work? Of course they do! We make sure that it's very easy to bring your stuff on the boat, including a fursuitlounge with basic supplies. We do understand that you maybe don't want to suit during the whole trip. Simply change out of suit and we keep guard. Also the outside decks are perfect for cooling down and waving at the many people on the side.

Transport

For this party you don't have to travel at all! We start in the biergarten of the Estrel hotel. Perfect for everyone who wants to party and see Berlin without traveling far. The boat is also ending the tour there so no worries to get back to the hotel. Before boarding it's already possible to receive your badge and lanyard so you can enter without waiting. For more information about that, check below in the FAQ.

Registration

The registration will open soon. After registration you have 14 days to pay the fees. When everything is done you are successfully registered and ready to board the ship.

The VIP upgrade is our name for the good old sponsor upgrade. With this upgrade you get not only special goodies, but you also support us so we can make it the best party ever! The upgrade is not limited to a maximum amount and can always be bought later in the user area. Downgrading is not possible but transferring it to someone else is. Just send us an email.

Before you register it is important that you are familiar with our Code of Conduct and Terms and Conditions. They explain the rules for keeping it a fun and awesome party.

If you have any questions left or can't wait until the party is starting, join then our Telegram group under @summerboat.

FAQ

We wanted to do something special for celebrating the 25th edition of Eurofurence and the 5th edition in Berlin, the cities that knows how to party! So why not on a boat? We can show you around Berlin via the beautiful Spree river and in the mean time chill, party and suit up. Basically we have a fursuitwalk (without walking) and party in one. Throw in a beautiful sunset and the best DJs and it's a perfect experience you don't want to miss.

Early Bird tickets cost €25. When there are no more available, you can purchase a Normal ticket for €35. There are only 100 early bird tickets available. We also offer a VIP upgrade for €15. You get a special gift and a super limited VIP badge, but you also help us to make the party even more awesome.
